# Anime Recommender
For all the anime lovers who craves to watch similiar anime they loved, but struggles. Here's your recommender, give your favourite anime and get five new recommendation.

## How does it work?

- Using cosine similarity algorithm, we made a similiarities.pkl from animes_dict.pkl
- When a anime is choosed and send as input, the range of its index compared with others in similarities.pkl and first 5 returned as output.
